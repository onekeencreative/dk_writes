Book Club
Discussion Questions


  • 1. How did you feel after reading this book? Did it impact your mood? If so, how?
  • 2. What did you think of Cali’s choice to go to Taos? Would you have made the same decision?
  • 3. Would you read a journal from your parents? How do you think that experience would have felt?
  • 4. How did the settings and locations impact the story? Are there any you wanted to visit?
  • 5. There is a lot of history of the American Southwest in this book. Were there places that surprised you or stood out?
  • 6. What did you think of Alex and Cali’s dynamic?
  • 7. If you could trade places with any character in the book, who would it be?
  • 8. Which characters did you relate to or empathize with the most?
  • 9. How did you feel about Cali’s hunt for information about her mother? Would you have gone to those lengths to find out your past?
  • 10. What did you think about the resolution of Cali’s search for Jamie?
  • 11. How did you feel about Cali’s reaction to Alex after learning about Jamie?
  • 12. What did you think of the ending of the book?
  • 13. Are there lingering questions or scenes from the book you're still thinking about?
