Debut Novel Coming Soon

Chasing Dust

Cali Clarke is a successful product designer in San Francisco, or at least she was until her mother unexpectedly died. Since then nothing has felt quite right. But when her hunt for answers about her mother turns up a house in New Mexico she never knew existed, things get even weirder. Knowing her only hope of making sense of her world is in that house, she decides to drop everything and go.

Chasing Dust follows Cali as she finds out more about her mother and herself through the lens of the American Southwest. She learns about a forgotten history of the country, distracts herself in the arms of a handsome artist and discovers that her own life isn’t as black and white as she thought.

Bio

Hi All, I'm Dani Keen!

For as long as I can remember I’ve been a writer. I was the kid walking around school with a notebook, jotting down stories. Now as an adult this love for storytelling has transitioned to novels. When someone picks up my book I want them to feel like they are on vacation. Explore new places, get lost in solving mysteries and let yourself fall in love.

I call sunny central Florida home, where I live with my husband and daughter. When I’m not writing, I love to spend time creating. Whether it be growing things in the garden, experimenting with recipes, or tinkering with different artistic mediums, I enjoy it all.
