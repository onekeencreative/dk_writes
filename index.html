Chasing Dust Coming
September 5th

A house in the desert
an old journal
& the secrets they keep

Cali Clarke is a successful product designer in San Francisco, who lives for work and happy hours with her best friend, or at least she did until her mother’s unexpected passing. Since then, nothing feels quite right. But when her efforts to settle her mother’s estate reveal a secret house in New Mexico, Cali finds herself with pressing questions. Knowing her only hope for answers is in that house, she drops everything to go.

The high desert is a complete change of pace, forcing Cali to down-shift from incessant emails to watching literal paint dry. It’s almost bearable, until she unearths her mother’s journal from thirty years prior. With answers about the past so close, Cali has to decide if the glimpses into her mother’s life are worth the pain they stir up in her own.

Cali's only reprieve comes from befriending a vivacious local coffee shop owner and the handsome and hopefully single bartender down the road. But when hidden family secrets surface, Cali discovers her own life isn’t as black and white as she thought.

With her friendships and career on the line, can Cali make peace with her past before she destroys her future?

Bio

Hi All, I'm Dani Keen!

For as long as I can remember I’ve been a writer. I was the kid walking around school with a notebook, jotting down stories. Now as an adult this love for storytelling has transitioned to novels. When someone picks up my book I want them to feel like they are on vacation. Explore new places, get lost in solving mysteries and let yourself fall in love.

I call sunny central Florida home, where I live with my husband and daughter. When I’m not writing, I love to spend time creating. Whether it be growing things in the garden, experimenting with recipes, or tinkering with different artistic mediums, I enjoy it all.
